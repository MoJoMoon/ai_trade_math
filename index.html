📐 AI Blueprint Design

🎯 Design Brief to AI

Using our math answers from the quiz, we'll prompt AI to create a house blueprint. Here's how we structure the prompt:

"Create a house blueprint for a 1,500 sq ft home with 3 bedrooms and 2 baths. Use these room allocations: [Your answers from Question 5]. The house should be 50ft × 30ft overall. Include accessibility features with a 1:12 ramp. Show room dimensions and layout."
